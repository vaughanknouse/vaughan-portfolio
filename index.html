About Me

Hi! I'm Vaughan Knouse, a passionate Full-Stack Web Developer based in Washington, DC. With over 10 years of experience in healthcare operations, I discovered my passion for improving processes through technology and web development.

I earned a Full-Stack Web Development certificate from The University of Texas at Austin, where I specialized in the MERN stack, SQL, and responsive web design. I am driven by building user-friendly applications that solve real-world problems.

Whether working independently or as part of a team, I bring a collaborative mindset, problem-solving skills, and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.
